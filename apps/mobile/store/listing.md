## Full description (Apple "Description" / Google "Full description", ≤4000 chars)

MailPoppy is the mobile app for your MailPoppy mailbox — professional email on your own domain,
with your messages kept private in your organisation's own cloud.

Sign in with the mailbox your organisation set up for you and get a fast, modern inbox wherever
you are.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Read, reply, forward and compose email on your own domain
• Attach photos and files, and open attachments you receive
• Organise mail across folders, and search your inbox
• Swipe to delete; manage your inbox with a clean, modern interface
• Get notified the moment new mail arrives
• Dark by design — easy on the eyes, day or night

PRIVATE BY ARCHITECTURE
MailPoppy doesn't run a mail service that sits between you and your email. Your mailbox lives in
your organisation's own cloud account, and your messages and password never pass through
MailPoppy's own systems. The app simply connects you to your mailbox.

WHO IT'S FOR
This app is for people whose email is hosted with MailPoppy. Your mailbox is created by your
organisation's administrator — open the app, sign in, and you're ready. (Setting up email on a
domain is done in the MailPoppy desktop app.)

Learn more at https://mailpoppy.com
